**Status:** In editorial revision.

This article extends the five-pillar framework into applied case studies, demonstrating how dataset characteristics influence:

- GWAS design and interpretation
- PRS construction and portability
- Rare variant aggregation strategies
- Reproducibility and bias considerations

The goal is to bridge principle and practice in real-world genomics research.
