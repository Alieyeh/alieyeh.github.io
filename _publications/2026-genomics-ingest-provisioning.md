**Status:** Technical manuscript in preparation.

This paper describes the design and implementation of an end-to-end genomics data facilitation pipeline within the DPUK Data Portal, covering:

- Metadata-driven dataset discovery (Genomics Metadata Matrix)
- Structured QC and privacy screening workflows
- Standardised cohort organisation for scalability
- Controlled and governed data provisioning mechanisms
- Integration with HPC-based analytical workflows

The framework aims to balance usability, reproducibility and governance in large-scale genomics research.
