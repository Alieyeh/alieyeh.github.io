**Status:** In editorial revision.

This article introduces a five-pillar framework to guide dataset selection in genomics research, focusing on:

- Governance and access constraints
- Data modality suitability (GWAS, WES, WGS)
- Cohort structure and ancestry considerations
- Statistical power implications
- Downstream methodological alignment

The piece aims to translate genomics complexity into actionable decision criteria for data scientists and applied researchers.
