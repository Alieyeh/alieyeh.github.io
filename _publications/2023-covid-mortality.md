Khodabakhsh P., Asadnia A., **Sarabandi Moghaddam A.**, Khademi M., Shakiba M., Maher A., Salehian E. (2023).

This study applied machine learning approaches to structured hospital datasets to predict in-hospital mortality among COVID-19 patients with diabetes. Models included decision trees and related classification strategies, with attention to clinical interpretability and feature relevance.
